About Us

JAEE is a professional manufacturer and exporter that is concerned with design,development and production of faucet, mixer and shower. We are located in Shuikou Town, Guangdong province, which is known of "Kingdom of Chinese sanitary ware". All of faucet and shower comply with international quality standards and are greatly appreciated in a variety of different markets throughout the world. JAEE covers an area of 4,000 sq.m, and the annual production capacity is more than 200,000 pcs.
